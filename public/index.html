About

FountTech API provides structured startup information through a modern RESTful interface. Access startup profiles, founders, industries, funding stages, products, services, and business data from the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

Who Is This API For?

FountTech API is designed for developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, incubators, accelerators, investors, and organizations seeking structured startup ecosystem data.

Developers

Integrate startup data into websites, mobile applications, dashboards, and business intelligence platforms.

Investors

Discover startups by industry, location, growth stage, and funding profile.

Incubators & Accelerators

Track startup portfolios, monitor ecosystem growth, and identify emerging ventures.

Researchers

Analyze startup trends, industries, employment, and business performance data.
